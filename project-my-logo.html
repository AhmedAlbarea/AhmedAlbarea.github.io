Why?

● Whether a big or small business, it's always important to have a brand identity. A logo is the best brand identity.
● It helps me to connect with my customers. and help my customers to easily remember me.
● When I see my logo's mockups, it gives me a very powerful positive charge that helps me to keep going, to work harder, and to pursue my big dreams.

Key challenges

Time constraint:
Designing a logo was just one of the steps of making my portfolio. I developed my portfolio website by myself and there were still a lot of things to do.

Stand out
I searched for letter A logos and found plenty of them. So it took time and effort to come up with this logo.

Toolkit

Ideate and Sketches.

After checking some ideas, I used crazy eight to sketch my ideas. But I wasn't satisfied with the results. So I postponed the process and kept working on my portfolio.

Inspiration.

I used a placeholder for my logo. it was an arrow shape that looks like a letter A. every time I open my editor to work on my portfolio, it actually gets my attention and looked good to me. So I decided to design my logo based on that.

More Sketches.

Setup and method

I used a grid method with some shapes to make sure that the logo is balanced and consistent, but that didn't work as I wanted.

The proper method

So I decided to use only two shapes ( triangle and a rectangle ) and it worked perfectly.

Necessary adjustment

After extracting the logo's shapes, it didn't look quite right. it looked short and fat. So I carefully applied the necessary adjustment with balance and consistency in mind.

Development reference

Instead of leaving the upper left cut empty or as a solid color, I changed it to binary code. I know it's not readable but that's ok because I did that to avoid white space and to make the logo looks better.

Brand name and Tagline
