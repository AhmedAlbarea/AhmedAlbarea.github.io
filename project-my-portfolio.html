Why?

● In the last course of my Google UX Design Professional Certificate., I was asked to provide a complete online portfolio. They introduced website builders to do that. At that time my web development knowledge wasn't good, so I tried website builders but I couldn't do what I wanted because they were limited. So I started learning Front-End development and built this portfolio.
● Since I started my career as a designer and developer, it was essential to have an online portfolio.

Key challenges

Do it better:
Learning web development and working on this portfolio simultaneously caused a lot of rework. When I finish working on a part, I learn how to do it better, so I do it better.

Do it my way
When I see how other developers do things, I don't just copy and paste. Sometimes it doesn't make sense to me even if it works for them, so I do things my way. that's why it took me a while to finish.
