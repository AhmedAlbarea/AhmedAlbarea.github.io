About Me 🤠

My journey to UX design and web development:

• I got my bachelor's degree in business management in 2011.

• After college, I got a job offer in the Kingdom of Bahrain. After three months, it didn't work out.

• After that, I got a temporary job as an inventory clerk in one of the largest groups in Bahrain called Ramez Group. I worked there for eight months.

• After that, I was recommended to work as a personnel affairs specialist in one of their industrial branches called Ramez Industrial Company. I worked there for three years.

• After that, I took my vacation to China. And unexpectedly, I settled there. I worked as an ESL teacher for four years.

• After that, I got my Google UX Design Professional Certificate and started working as a freelance UX designer till now.

• Besides being a freelance UX designer, I'm also learning web development (HTML, CSS, javascript, php). I developed this portfolio myself.

• For more details, contact me or check my resume.
